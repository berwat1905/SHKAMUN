About SHKAMUN

SHKAMUN is a Model United Nations conference organized by Şehit Halil Kantarcı Anadolu İmam Hatip High School, designed to provide young people with the opportunity to develop their skills in international diplomacy, negotiation, and social responsibility.

This conference offers participants the chance to enhance their abilities to think from the perspectives of different countries and organizations, engage in effective discussions, and propose innovative solutions to global challenges.

SHKAMUN enables young people not only to gain knowledge about global issues but also to build new friendships, develop leadership skills, and gain valuable experience in international diplomacy.

Theme: "Choose Diplomacy, Shape History" - A symbol of how young people can shape history through diplomacy.
